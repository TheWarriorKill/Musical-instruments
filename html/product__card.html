description

The OM-28 Modern Deluxe is a new take on Martin Vintage. This guitar is packed with custom features and modern technology that you have to see, feel, and hear for yourself. The vintage appointments include a solid Sitka spruce VINTAGE TONE SYSTEM® (VTS) top, a dovetail neck joint, and natural protein glue construction that give it the rich, fully aged Martin tone that has inspired generations. The look is unlike anything in our production line to date with European flamed maple binding, stylish gold frets, gold open-gear tuners, and a unique 1930s style script logo inlaid in pearl on the headstock. Some ultra-modern features include Liquidmetal® bridge pins with red dots and a composite carbon fiber bridgeplate that boosts volume. And we’ve included a new neck shape that is slightly asymmetrical for maximum hand comfort up and down the fretboard. This guitar is anything but standard. Included with this beautiful guitar is a deluxe hardshell case.

specification

The Martin OM-28E Modern Deluxe is an exceptional acoustic guitar that combines vintage aesthetics with modern innovations. It boasts a range of custom features and advanced technology that must be experienced firsthand to truly appreciate. Retaining the classic Martin Vintage tone, the guitar features a solid Sitka spruce VINTAGE TONE SYSTEM® (VTS) top, a traditional dovetail neck joint, and natural protein glue construction. These elements contribute to its rich, fully aged Martin sound that has been cherished for generations. In terms of appearance, the OM-28E Modern Deluxe stands out from the rest of Martin's lineup. It showcases European flamed maple binding, elegant gold frets, gold open-gear tuners, and a striking 1930s style script logo inlaid in pearl on the headstock. These design elements add a touch of sophistication and uniqueness to the instrument. Combining vintage and modern elements, the guitar incorporates innovative features such as Liquidmetal® bridge pins with red dots for enhanced resonance and a composite carbon fiber bridgeplate that boosts volume. Additionally, the neck is designed with a slightly asymmetrical shape, providing optimal comfort and playability across the entire fretboard. The Martin OM-28E Modern Deluxe sets itself apart from standard guitars, offering a remarkable playing experience. It comes complete with a deluxe hardshell case for added protection and convenience.
